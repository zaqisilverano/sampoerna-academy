Teaching and Learning Methods

The teaching and learning process places students at the center of the learning process. They will experience:

 

Student-centered and inquiry-based learning Students learn to seek knowledge and information through questioning. They would:

 

Gain useful and applicable knowledge,

 

Find solutions, and

 

Become problem solvers.

 

Blended and digital learning Students learn through via digital media and face-to-face classrooms with teachers.

 

Project-based scaffolded learning Students will have real-world application of their knowledge by:

 

Exploring real-world problems,

 

Working collaboratively,

 

Communicating ideas, and

 

Becoming creative innovators.
